Under 11s
Progression from U10s: Kicking is introduced, along with more complex scrums.
Key Rules:
Teams: Maximum 9 players per team.
Pitch Size: Maximum 70m x 43m.
Game Duration: Maximum 20 minutes per half.
Gameplay:
Scrums: Contested hooking is introduced with scrums of 3 players, focusing on correct technique but not yet pushing.
Kicking: Allowed strategically, including for territory and from restarts. The team that has been scored against has the choice of taking the restarting kick or receiving it.
Rucks: Rucks expand to 2 v 2
Tackling, rucks, and mauls become more dynamic.
Development Focus: Enhances tactical awareness and technical skills, preparing players for more complex play.
